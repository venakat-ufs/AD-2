Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

Why is residential property maintenance important?

Residential maintenance is essential to ensure that your home remains in good condition, comfortable, and safe. Proper maintenance helps preserve the value of your property, prevents costly repairs, and ensures your home remains a welcoming space for you or your tenants.

How much do residential property maintenance services cost?

Our services are tailored to your specific needs and budget. Contact us for a free quote based on your property's requirements.

Can residential maintenance services be customized to my needs?

Yes, absolutely! We work closely with you to assess your unique requirements and develop a customized maintenance plan tailored to your needs and budget.

What kind of property maintenance services does United Field Services offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of services including repairs, cleaning, landscaping, HVAC maintenance, electrical work, painting, and more. We also provide rehab & renovation, property preservation, and inspection services.
